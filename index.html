About Samco

Samco is a British made hose made to improve performance by replacing the stock hoses with a improved version and all made by hand at a factory based in Wales.

Samco supplies multiple motor sports with their performance hoses, from motor cross jumping over huge ramps to moto GP with the extreme high speed cornering, to rally cross in high BHP cars. they also can be used on normal vehicles to like road bikes cars and tractors.

Samco have lots of brilliant distributors that will get you're products to you on time and with the best service they can.


Samco products come with a life time warranty this makes the hoses you get not just an upgrade that will last a few years it will last the life time with the products but some products don't come with a life time warranty because of the products location on the vehicle and what's going through the hose.
